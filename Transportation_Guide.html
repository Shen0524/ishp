Transportation Guide

To Howard Civil Service International House (福華文教會館)

Hotel Address: No. 30, Section 3, Xinsheng South Road, Da’an District, Taipei City, Taiwan
Phone: +886-2-7712-2323



From Taoyuan International Airport (TPE)

Option 1: MRT + Taxi
  • Take the Taoyuan Airport MRT to Taipei Main Station (35–40 min, NT$160).
  • From Taipei Main Station, take a taxi to the hotel (15 min, NT$220–250).
Option 2: MRT All the Way
  • From Taipei Main Station, take the Red Line to Chiang Kai-shek Memorial Hall (R08/G10).
  • Transfer to the Green Line and go to Taipower Building (G08).
  • Exit 2, walk 800 meters to Xinhai & Xinsheng S. Rd., then turn left.
  • Total time: 60–70 minutes | Cost: NT$185
Option 3: Taxi
  • Direct from airport to hotel.
  • Time: 45–60 minutes | Cost: NT$1,200–1,500

From Taipei Songshan Airport (TSA)

Option 1: Taxi
  • Direct from airport to hotel.
  • Time: 20–30 minutes | Cost: NT$200–250
Option 2: MRT
  • Take the Brown Line (BR13) to Nanjing-Fuxing (BR11/G16).
  • Transfer to the Green Line and go to Taipower Building (G08).
  • Exit 2, walk 800 meters to the hotel.
  • Total time: 40–50 minutes | Cost: NT$50

Walking Directions from MRT Taipower Building (G08)

  • Exit 2: Turn left onto Xinhai Road.
  • Walk 800 meters (~10–15 min) to the intersection with Xinsheng South Road.
  • Turn left; the hotel will be on your left.
